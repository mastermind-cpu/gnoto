Our Procedure

I went undertook several procedures to come up with the flower vessel.

1. Collecting bottle tops

I took 2 days collecting plastic bottles from homes,neighbours and the community.

2. Cleaning

I washed the plastic bottle because they were very dirty since they were rubbish. I let them dry and they were ready for use.

3. Cutting and designing

I cut the [lastic bottles into the shapes you're seeing to give them the best look out of my hands.

4. Aligning the materials

I collected loam soil and bought the flowers. I put the flower and the loam soil in the flower vessels. Finally I added water and fertilizers for proper growth.

5. Placing in the desired place.

After 3-4 days the flower vessels are put in the desired place for decoration.
